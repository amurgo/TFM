Notice of Decision 

	Rule 40(3) of the Tribunal Procedure (Upper Tribunal) Rules 2008 (SI 2008/2698 as amended) applies to this decision.

	These appeals concern a Human rights claim.  Both parties were agreed that the judge fell into error in terms of the standard and burden of proof set out at paragraph 250 of the determination requiring the decision to be set aside with none of the factual findings to stand. 

	By consent the appeal to the Upper Tribunal is allowed as follows:

the First-tier Tribunal decision is affected by error of law.

the First-tier Tribunal decision is set aside, with no factual findings preserved. 

the appeals are remitted to the First-tier Tribunal to be reheard de novo by a judge other than Judge Raymond.
