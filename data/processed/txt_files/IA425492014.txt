                            NOTICE OF WITHDRAWAL

  Pursuant to Rule 17 (5) of the Tribunal Procedure (Upper Tribunal) Rules
                                    2008

 1. The appellant's application for permission to appeal was granted by  an
    Immigration  Judge   in   the   First-tier   Tribunal   which   remains
    undetermined.

 2. By email dated 19 May 2015, the appellant notified the  Upper  Tribunal
    that she wished to withdraw her appeal.

 3. Pursuant to Rule 17(2) of the Tribunal Procedure (Upper Tribunal) Rules
    2008, a notice of withdrawal will  not  take  effect  until  the  Upper
    Tribunal  consents  to  the  withdrawal,  except  in  relation  to   an
    application for permission to appeal.   My  consent  is  not  therefore
    necessary.

OUTCOME

    The appeal is withdrawn.
