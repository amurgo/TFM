                                  DECISION

 1. The Appellants appeal, with permission, against  the  decision  of  the
    First-tier Tribunal (the "FtT") which dismissed their appeals against a
    series of inter-related decisions of the Respondent, dated 03  December
    2013.


 2. By letter dated 16 September 2014, the Appellants' solicitors  informed
    the Upper Tribunal that their clients wished to withdraw  the  appeals.
    The only other material information available to the Tribunal, supplied
    by Mr Harrison on behalf of the Respondent, is that  the Appellants are
    making arrangements to leave the United Kingdom.

 3. In the circumstances, in accordance  with  rule  17  of  the  Tribunals
    (Upper Tribunal)  Rules  of  Procedure  2008,  I  grant  permission  to
    withdraw the appeals.

 4. The decision of the FtT is hereby affirmed.
