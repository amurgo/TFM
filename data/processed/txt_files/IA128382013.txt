   NOTICE OF ABANDONMENT UNDER RULE 17(2) OF THE TRIBUNAL PROCEDURE (UPPER
                            TRIBUNAL) RULES 2008


1.   In a letter sent to the Tribunal on behalf of the the  appellant  dated
    5 January 2014, her representatives have indicated that  the  appellant
    wishes to withdraw her appeal before the Upper Tribunal.  I  have  been
    shown a letter from the respondent dated 6 December 2013 from which  it
    appears that the appellant  has  subsequently  been  granted  leave  to
    remain.
 2.      On this basis, I consent to the withdrawal of the appeal.
3.   This notice serves as notification of a withdrawal under  Rule  17(sub-
    paragraph 2) of the Tribunal Procedure (Upper Tribunal) Rules 2008.

Signed:                                 Date: 22 January 2014
