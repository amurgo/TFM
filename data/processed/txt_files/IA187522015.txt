                                DETERMINATION

 1. The appellant appeals against a decision by First-tier  Tribunal  Judge
    Pullig, promulgated on 22 February 2017, dismissing his appeal for want
    of jurisdiction.  The UT indicated in its grant of permission dated  18
    December 2017 that unless either party  explained  its  opposition  the
    following would apply.  Nothing is on file from the parties within  the
    time specified.

 2. Judge Pullig applied the law as then understood, but in light of Khan v
    SSHD [2017] EWCA Civ 1755 that must now be considered an error of  law.
    The decision of the FtT is set aside. The case is remitted to  the  FtT
    for fresh hearing.

 3. No anonymity direction has been requested or made.
