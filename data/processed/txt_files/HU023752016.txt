                      RULING AND NOTICE OF ABANDONMENT


   1. The appellant's passport reveals  that  the  applicant  has  left  the
      United Kingdom pending the conclusion of his appeal before  the  Upper
      Tribunal.
   2. By operation of section 92 (8) of  the  Nationality,  Immigration  and
      Asylum Act, 2002 as substituted by s. 17 of the Immigration Act  2014,
      an appeal brought by a person while he is in the United Kingdom  shall
      be treated as abandoned if the appellant leaves the United Kingdom.
   3. The appeal was not certified under ss. 94 (1) or (7) or 94B.


  RULING


      The appellant's appeal is treated as abandoned.
