                            NOTICE OF WITHDRAWAL


1.   Ms Fijiwala sought to withdraw the Secretary of State's appeal against
    decision by First-tier Tribunal Judge Lenier allowing the appeal of the
    respondent under the 2006 EEA Regulations.
2.   Consent of the Upper Tribunal is required for a party to withdraw its
    case.  Accordingly, with my consent, and pursuant to rule 17(5) of the
    Tribunal Procedure (Upper Tribunal) Rules 2008, this appeal is recorded
    as "Withdrawn with the consent of the Upper Tribunal".

3.   The effect of this is that the proceedings before the Upper Tribunal
    are at end.  There is no appeal before the Upper Tribunal and the
    decision of the First-tier Tribunal in relation to this respondent
    shall stand.
