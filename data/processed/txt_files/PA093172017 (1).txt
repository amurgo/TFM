                             NOTICE OF DECISION

 1. Both subparagraphs of rule  40(3)  of  the  Tribunal  Procedure  (Upper
    Tribunal) Rules 2008 (SI 2008/2698 as amended) apply to this decision.

 2. The decision of the First-tier Tribunal sent out on 16 November 2018 is
    erroneous in law.  I set it aside.

 3. The appeal is remitted to be determined by the First-tier  Tribunal  de
    novo at an oral hearing before a judge other than Judge A Walker.

 4. As the appellant is now resident in Widnes, the appeal  is  transferred
    for hearing at the Manchester Hearing Centre.
