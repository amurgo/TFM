                            NOTICE OF ABANDONMENT

 1. The SSHD appealed against a determination by First-tier Tribunal  Judge
    Fox, promulgated on 26 March 2014, allowing the appeal by  Mr  Zhengtao
    Cai (a citizen of China, born on 30 July 1995) against refusal  of  his
    application as a student under the Points Based System (PBS).

 2. Mr Matthews advised that the appellant has left  the  UK,  voluntarily,
    but in compliance with removal directions  made  by  the  SSHD  at  his
    request.

 3. Under reference to section 104(4) of  the  Nationality,  Immigration  &
    Asylum Act 2002 and to  Rule  17A  of  the  Tribunal  Procedure  (Upper
    Tribunal) Rules 2008 the appeal is treated as abandoned.
