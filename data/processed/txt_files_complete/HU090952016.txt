                            DECISION AND REMITTAL

 1. The ground on which the appellant has  permission  to  appeal  to  this
    Tribunal is that Judge Dean dismissed the appeal under the  Immigration
    Rules and made no decision on the  human  rights  ground,  whereas  her
    jurisdiction was limited to the human rights grounds.  There is no r 24
    response.

 2. In accordance with the proposal made in my letter to the parties  dated
    5 March 2018, I now set aside the decision of Judge Dean for  error  of
    law and remit the appellant's appeal to  the  First-tier  Tribunal  for
    determination by a different judge.
