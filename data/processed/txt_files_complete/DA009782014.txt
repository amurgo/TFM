                         NOTIFICATION OF WITHDRAWAL

The appellant was  granted  permission  to  appeal  to  the  Upper  Tribunal
against a determination by Designated Judge Murray,  dismissing  his  appeal
against deportation.  In a communication dated 26 June  2015  and  confirmed
at the hearing, he sought to withdraw his appeal to the Upper Tribunal.

In terms of the Tribunal Procedure (Upper Tribunal)  Rules  2008,  Rule  17,
the Upper Tribunal consents to withdrawal.  The determination of the  First-
tier Tribunal accordingly stands.
