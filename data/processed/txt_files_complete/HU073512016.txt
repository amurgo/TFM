                            Notice of Withdrawal



 1. The First-tier Tribunal allowed the appeal of Ms Paca under  Article  8
    ECHR in a determination dated 11 July 2017.

 2. The Secretary of State for the Home Department applied  for  permission
    to appeal the decision and permission was granted on 26 July 2017.

 3. In a letter dated  5  October  2017  the  respondent  gives  notice  of
    withdrawal of her case before the Upper Tribunal. She concedes that  Ms
    Paca qualifies for leave under Article 8 ECHR  in  line  with  her  own
    published guidance.

 4. The Tribunal consents to the withdrawal.

 5. The decision of the First-tier Tribunal stands and Ms Paca's appeal  is
    allowed under Article 8 ECHR.



Signed: [pic]                     Dated: 5 October 2017
Upper Tribunal Judge Pitt
