                            NOTICE OF WITHDRAWAL

       This was an appeal to  the  Court  of  Appeal  against  the  decision
    promulgated on 23 May 2014 of Judge Aitken, upholding the  decision  of
    First-tier Tribunal  Doyle  which  dismissed  the  appellant's  Tier  4
    student appeal.

 1. The appellant wrote to the Tribunal on 19 August 2014  indicating  that
    he wished to withdraw his appeal.

 2. In line with Rule 17 (1) (a) of The Tribunal Procedure (Upper Tribunal)
    Rules 2008 the Tribunal consents to the withdrawal of  the  appellant's
    appeal to the Upper Tribunal.

 3. The appeal to the Upper Tribunal is withdrawn. The  decision  of  Judge
    Aitken and that of First-tier Tribunal Doyle stand.
