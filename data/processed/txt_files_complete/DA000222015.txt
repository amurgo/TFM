                         NOTIFICATION OF WITHDRAWAL

 1. On 5 June 2015 permission was granted to appeal against a determination
    by First-tier  Tribunal  Judge  R  M  M  Wallace  dated  25  May  2015,
    dismissing the appellant's deportation  appeal.   By  letter  dated  21
    August 2015  the  appellant,  through  his  representatives,  seeks  to
    withdraw his appeal.

 2. In terms of the Tribunal Procedure (Upper Tribunal)  Rules  2008,  Rule
    17, the appeal is recorded as having been withdrawn, with  the  consent
    of the Upper Tribunal.  The determination of  the  First-tier  Tribunal
    therefore stands.
