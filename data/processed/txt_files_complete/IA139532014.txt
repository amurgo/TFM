                            NOTICE OF WITHDRAWAL

 1. This was an appeal against the  decision  of  the  First-tier  Tribunal
    (Judge Gibbs) promulgated on 26 November 2014.

 2. Permission to appeal to the Upper Tribunal was granted on 9 March 2015.



 3. The appellant now seeks to withdraw her  appeal  as  she  accepts  that
    Judge Gibbs did not err in declining jurisdiction of her  human  rights
    appeal; Amirteymour and others (EEA appeals; human rights) [2015]  UKUT
    00466 (IAC) applied.

 4. In line with Rule 17 (1) (a) of The Tribunal Procedure (Upper Tribunal)
    Rules 2008 the Tribunal consents to the withdrawal of  the  appellant's
    appeal to the Upper Tribunal.

 5. The appeal to the Upper Tribunal is  withdrawn.  The  decision  of  the
    First-tier Tribunal stands.
