                       NOTICE OF ABANDONMENT OF APPEAL

By letter dated 12 November 2015 the  appellant's  solicitors  informed  the
Tribunal that the appellant had been given leave to  remain  in  the  United
Kingdom.

It  follows  that,  pursuant  to  section  104(4A)   of   the   Nationality,
Immigration and Asylum Act 2002, this appeal is abandoned.
