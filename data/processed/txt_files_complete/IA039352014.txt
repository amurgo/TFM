                          DETERMINATION AND REASONS


 1. This is the Secretary of State's challenge to  the  decision  of  Judge
    Hindson made following a hearing at Bradford on 2nd May 2014.

 2.  In  his  decision  Judge  Hindson  dismissed  the  appeal  under   the
    Immigration Rules but allowed it on human rights grounds.

 3. The Secretary of State sought and was granted permission on  23rd  June
    2014 to challenge Judge Hindson's decision.  However at the hearing Mrs
    Pettersen confirmed that she was withdrawing her challenge.

Decision

 4. The judge's decision stands and Mr Banda's appeal is allowed.
