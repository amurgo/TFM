                          DETERMINATION BY CONSENT


 1. Upon hearing the parties and by consent it is ordered pursuant to  Rule
    39 of the Tribunal Procedure (Upper Tribunal) Rules 2008,

      a) the decision of the First-tier Tribunal dated 31 December  2013  by
         First-tier Tribunal Judge T Hollingworth is set aside,

      b) the appellant's appeal against the decision of the Entry  Clearance
         Officer dated 19 December 2012 is allowed under  paragraph  281  of
         the Immigration Rules,

      c) the Entry Clearance Officer is directed to grant to  the  appellant
         entry clearance.

 2. Having regard to the agreement between the parties and the  requirement
    for the appeal in the Upper Tribunal to be disposed of, this appeal  is
    allowed.
