                                  DECISION

 1. This is a decision to which Rule 40(3)(a)  of  the  Tribunal  Procedure
    (Upper Tribunal) Rules 2008 applies.

 2. At the hearing, Mr Clarke conceded as a matter of fact that at the date
    of the decision under appeal the appellant  had  completed  ten  years'
    lawful residence in the United Kingdom.

 3. The decision of the First-tier Tribunal is set aside and we re-make the
    decision.  We allow the appeal.
