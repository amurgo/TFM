                            NOTICE OF WITHDRAWAL



 1. This was an appeal against the  decision  of  the  First-tier  Tribunal
    (Judge N J Bennett) promulgated on 19 June 2014.  Permission to  appeal
    to the Upper Tribunal has not been granted.

 2. The appellant wrote to the Tribunal on 15 August 2014  indicating  that
    he wished to withdraw her appeal.

 3. In line with Rule 17 (1) (a) of The Tribunal Procedure (Upper Tribunal)
    Rules 2008 the Tribunal consents to the withdrawal of  the  appellant's
    appeal to the Upper Tribunal.

 4. The appeal to the Upper Tribunal is  withdrawn.  The  decision  of  the
    First-tier Tribunal stands.
