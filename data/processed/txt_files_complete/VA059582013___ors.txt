                                    ORDER

 1. Mr McVeety disclosed that, pursuant to further decisions of  the  Entry
    Clearance Officer, all three Appellants secured visas and have  visited
    the United Kingdom.   In  passing,  while  the  father  of  the  family
    returned to Bangladesh following  his  visit,  the  two  children  have
    remained here, having submitted asylum claims.

 2. In these circumstances, Mr McVeety, on behalf  of  the  Appellant  (the
    Entry  Clearance  Officer)  applied  for  permission  to  withdraw  his
    client's case.  I duly granted  permission  under  Rule  17(1)  of  the
    Tribunal Procedure (Upper Tribunal) Rules 2008.
