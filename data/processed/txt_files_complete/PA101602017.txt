                            DECISION AND REMITTAL

 1. After considering the grounds of application, the grant  of  permission
    to appeal and the response under ruled 24, I wrote to the parties on  4
    December 2018 proposing the course of action below and indicating  that
    submissions to the contrary would be considered if received  within  21
    days.  No response has been received.

 2. I now set aside the decision of the First-tier Tribunal  for  error  of
    law as identified in the grant of permission and remit the appeal for a
    fresh hearing before the First-tier Tribunal.
