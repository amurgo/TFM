                       NOTICE OF WITHDRAWAL OF APPEAL

By letter dated 5 November 2015  the  appellants'  solicitors  informed  the
Tribunal that the appellants wished to withdraw their appeals  because  they
have been given, and have used, visitors' visas for the United Kingdom.

Pursuant to rule 17(2) of the  Tribunal  Procedure  (Upper  Tribunal)  Rules
2008 I give permission to the appellants to withdraw their appeals.

This is a Notice pursuant to rule 17(5) of  the  Tribunal  Procedure  (Upper
Tribunal) Rules 2008 to the parties that the appeals are  withdrawn  at  the
appellants' request and with the permission of the Upper Tribunal.
