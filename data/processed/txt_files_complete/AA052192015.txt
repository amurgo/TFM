                       NOTICE OF WITHDRAWAL OF APPEAL

By letter  dated  25  May  2016  the  appellant's  solicitors  informed  the
Tribunal that the appellant  wished  to  withdraw  the  appeal  because  the
Respondent had promised unequivocally to  withdraw  the  decision  that  had
resulted in the appeal being brought.

Pursuant to rule 17(2) of the  Tribunal  Procedure  (Upper  Tribunal)  Rules
2008 I give permission to the appellant to withdraw his appeal.

This is a Notice pursuant to rule 17(5) of  the  Tribunal  Procedure  (Upper
Tribunal) Rules 2008 to the parties that the  appeal  is  withdrawn  at  the
appellant's request and with the permission of the Upper Tribunal.
