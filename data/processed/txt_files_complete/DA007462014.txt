                         NOTIFICATION OF WITHDRAWAL

 1. The appellant appealed against a determination by  First-tier  Tribunal
    Judge McGrade, promulgated on 14 August 2014.

 2. The Presenting  Officer  advised  that  the  respondent  issued  a  new
    decision notice on 26 January 2015, which carries a  right  of  appeal.
    She sought to withdraw the respondent's case.  Mr Winter concurred.

 3. In terms of the Tribunal Procedure (Upper Tribunal)  Rules  2008,  Rule
    17, the appeal is recorded as  having  been  withdrawn  orally  at  the
    hearing, with the consent of the Upper Tribunal.
