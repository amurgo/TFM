                            NOTICE OF ABANDONMENT


1.   The resumed hearing of the appellant's appeal was due to take place on
    20 June 2016 before the Upper Tribunal.  By way of a letter dated 16
    June 2016, the respondent has notified the Upper Tribunal that the
    appellant and her dependants will be granted 5 years Humanitarian
    Protection.

2.   In the circumstances, by virtue of Section 104(4A) of the Nationality,
    Immigration and Asylum Act 2002, the Upper Tribunal will treat the
    appellant's appeal brought under section 82(1) as abandoned.

3.   The effect of this is that the proceedings before the Upper Tribunal
    are at an end.
