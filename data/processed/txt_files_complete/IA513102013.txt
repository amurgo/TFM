                            NOTICE OF WITHDRAWAL

      1. The appellant, a citizen of  the  Philippines,  has  permission  to
         appeal against  the  First-tier  Tribunal  decision  upholding  the
         respondent's decision to make removal directions with no in-country
         right of appeal.

      2. By a letter dated  Wednesday  2  September  2015,  the  appellant's
         representatives stated that they were instructed  to  withdraw  the
         appeal because the appellant would be submitting a new  application
         due to a change in her circumstances.

      3. Pursuant to paragraph 17 of The Tribunal Procedure (Upper Tribunal)
         Rules 2008 (as amended), the permission of the  Upper  Tribunal  is
         required for such withdrawal.

      4. The Tribunal consents to that withdrawal.
