                            NOTICE OF WITHDRAWAL


 1. In a decision promulgated on 20 February 2014,  Deputy  Upper  Tribunal
    Judge Mailer found an error of law in the determination  of  First-tier
    Tribunal  Judge Kaler and set  aside  that  decision.  The  appeal  was
    listed for hearing in order for the decision to be re-made.

 2. The appellant wrote to the Tribunal on 12 August 2014  indicating  that
    he wished to withdraw his appeal.

 3. In line with Rule 17 (1) (a) of The Tribunal Procedure (Upper Tribunal)
    Rules 2008 the Tribunal consents to the withdrawal of  the  appellant's
    appeal to the Upper Tribunal.

 4. The appeal to the Upper Tribunal is withdrawn. The appellant no  longer
    challenges the decision of the respondent dated 20 July  2013  refusing
    an EEA registration certificate.
