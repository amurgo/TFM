                                                          Abandonment of
   Appeal

   1. This is an appeal by  Secretary  of  State  for  the  Home  Department
      against the decision  of  the  First-tier  Tribunal  17  January  2013
      dismissing the appellant's appeal pursuant to  the  Immigration  Rules
      and allowing her appeal her  appeal  pursuant  to  Article  8  of  the
      European Convention on Human Rights.


   2. There was no appearance for the appellant on the date of the hearing.


   3.  At  the  hearing,  Ms  Isherwood  for  the  respondent  provided   an
      application details form which indicates that the appellant  has  been
      granted entry clearance as a spouse for 33  months  from  17  December
      2013.  There was also a letter from the appellant's  husband  received
      on 17 March 2014 which states that  the  appellant  has  been  granted
      leave to enter the United Kingdom following a  fresh  application  and
      she has entered the United Kingdom.


   4. In accordance with Rule 17A of the Upper Tribunal Rules, the appeal is
      abandoned.
