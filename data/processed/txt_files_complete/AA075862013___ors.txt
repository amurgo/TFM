                         NOTIFICATION OF WITHDRAWAL

 1. These are appeals against a determination by FtT Judge Dennis, dated  3
    October 2013.  This notification follows the UT's note  and  directions
    dated 30 January 2014.  The appeals were  listed  for  further  hearing
    today.  However, the solicitors for  the  appellants  have  advised  in
    writing that their clients have been  granted  discretionary  leave  to
    remain and seek to withdraw their case from the UT.

 2. In terms of the Tribunal Procedure (Upper Tribunal)  Rules  2008,  Rule
    17, the appeals are recorded as having been withdrawn, with the consent
    of the Upper Tribunal.
