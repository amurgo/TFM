                       NOTICE OF WITHDRAWAL OF APPEAL
On 7 May 2015 Upper Tribunal Judge Freeman gave the Appellant permission  to
appeal a decision of the First-tier Tribunal dismissing her  appeal  against
a decision of the Respondent refusing her leave  to  remain  in  the  United
Kingdom as a Tier 1 (Entrepreneur) Migrant.
By letter dated 28 August 2015 the appellant informed the Tribunal that  she
had decided to withdraw her appeal.
Pursuant to rule 17(2) of the  Tribunal  Procedure  (Upper  Tribunal)  Rules
2008 I give permission to the appellant to withdraw her appeal.
This is a Notice pursuant to rule 17(5) of  the  Tribunal  Procedure  (Upper
Tribunal) Rules 2008 to the parties that the  appeal  is  withdrawn  at  the
appellant's request and with the permission of the Upper Tribunal.
