                       NOTICE OF ABANDONMENT OF APPEAL

    The appellant has an  appeal  outstanding  before  the  Upper  Tribunal
    listed on the 19th December 2017. By an email dated 4th  December  2017
    the appellant has stated that he wishes to withdraw the appeal  because
    he has left the United Kingdom  and  does  not  intend  to  pursue  his
    appeal. Therefore the appeal is to be treated as abandoned pursuant  to
    Section 92(8) of the 2002 Act subject to  any  representations  to  the
    contrary made by the Secretary of State if there is reason  to  believe
    that the appellant has not left the UK.
