    NOTICE OF WITHDRAWAL PURSUANT TO RULE 17 OF THE 2008 PROCEDURE RULES

 1. The respondent is Mr Yongfu Jiang. He is a citizen  of  China  and  his
    date of birth is 17th November 1977.  I  shall  refer  to  him  as  the
    appellant as he was before the FtT.

2.   The appellant has been in  the  UK  since  January  2001.  He  made  an
    application under the long residence Rules which  was  refused  by  the
    Secretary of State on 7th March 2014. He appealed against the  decision
    and his  appeal  was  allowed  by  Judge  of  the  First-tier  Tribunal
    Griffiths on the grounds that the decision was not in  accordance  with
    the law.

3.   The Secretary of State submitted grounds seeking permission  to  appeal
    and permission to  appeal  was  granted  by  Judge  of  the  First-tier
    Tribunal Nicholson on 23rd January 2015.

4.   At the hearing before me Mr Jarvis representing the Secretary of  State
    made an application orally to withdraw the Secretary of  State's  case.
    There was no  objection  to  this  by  Mr  Toal.  I  consented  to  the
    withdrawal the Secretary of State's case.
